Mayan Counting

Addition and Multiplication using only 1s, 5s, and 0/20s.

The Mayans used a base-20 system for calculating large numbers, and that system used a combination of 0s (shell), 1s (dot), and 5s (bar). They also would count vertically, with the next higher block being a 20 fold increase. The first block is face value of dots/bars/shell, the second block would be 20 times its combination of dots and bars. The third block, 20 times 20 times larger.

I devised this little game to test your knowledge of mathematics combined with the Mayan method of counting. If you are having difficulty understanding the maths, go here for more in-depth explanation.
